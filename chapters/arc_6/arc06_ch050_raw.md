At the sound of her cold voice, Subaru literally felt his heart freeze.

Subaru: [――――]

Looking back at the front of the room, Ram stood still at the entrance, her light pink irises ablaze, quietly boring into Subaru.

Though Subaru had only came in contact with her a few times before, he had rarely ever seen her reveal such emotion on her face―― in that Ram, there was a fervor that easily betrayed that impression.

Subaru: [Nh, uh…]

Ram: [What are you getting so flustered for? Ram asked you a question. Answering is Barusu’s… No, answering is your duty[1].]

Subaru: [I-I am, just…]

Ram: [Just?]

Promptly, Subaru’s head began revolving as he attempted to explain himself.

He needed to move his tongue somehow, curse his sluggish mind for its initial response and meet this situation with a silver tongue.

However, in the first place, in addition to having been thrown into this unexpected turmoil, before this had occurred, Subaru had not―― he had yet to even process how Meili’s corpse had disappeared.

He had not even prepared the words to talk his way out of things, he was not even able to prepare to come to grips to what was happening right before his eyes.

All that Subaru was able to do, was to open and shut his mouth awkwardly as he tried his best to not to say anything improper.

Even though Subaru’s manner now supported his suspicious behavior louder than any words could.

Subaru: [――――]

Ram narrowed her eyes at Subaru’s silence. The coldness in her gaze, which he had already thought to be at the level of absolute zero, fell even further. It almost felt as if he had been assailed by a sharp, bone-chilling blizzard.

While desperately trying to keep his knees from trembling, Subaru slowly started to understand, too little too late.

――Had he been set up?

When Meili’s body had not been found, the meeting at the dinner table had been a bluff.

When they had reported that Meili’s body had not been discovered, Subaru had been relieved that he had escaped from a chance of being blamed.

Desperately searching in vain, they had not uncovered the not-so-well-hidden body of the little girl. His bad luck had prevailed even in these unnatural circumstances, Subaru had accepted the convenient truth, putting off any niggling doubts that had concerned him then.

And that had resulted in him being caught up in this uncouth act.

Hadn’t Subaru also repeatedly seen this in various TV shows?

In the final scene, the murderer who had so meticulously put together his perfect plan, would return to the crime scene and give himself away as the police and the detectives would be closely watching the crime scene. And because of that, they would give away that they had committed the crime by showing themselves as being the smoking gun. That kind of comedic-ish moment.

Somewhere in his mind, Subaru had thought that, were he in that position, he would never show such stupidity.

Any connoisseur of mysteries would take note of the sheer foolishness of the dumb criminal that would slip up at the very end.

He had acted completely the same way―― rather, he had been worse, for his shock of being caught up in this act had made him quite a miserable sight to behold.

Ram: [――Fake.]

Ram’s quiet mutter barrelled into him as he was stricken by his own defective actions.

With just that single word, Subaru’s brittle heart crumbled and sunk in his chest, as if he had been shell-shocked by a mortar attack. As soon as he had heard it, his face had turned as white as a sheet, his lips trembling. Ram let out a sigh.

Ram: [So, you have nothing to say to defend yourself. I guess that is proof that even you yourself, were aware of just how botched your acting is. You did not prepare well enough when disguising yourself as him. There are limits when it comes to not being diligent, don’t you agree?]

Without hiding the contempt in her voice, Ram began explaining why she had suspected Subaru.

Fakeness, poor acting, and showing insufficient understanding towards “Natsuki Subaru” had all been pointed out by her. Not only was Subaru lost for words, but his heart felt as if it was going to crack, and the flow of his blood ached with each heartbeat.

In fact, Subaru bit his lower lip, and blood started to stream out, accompanied by a sharp stinging pain.

A fake, fake, fake, fake, fake, fake, fake, fake, fake, fake, fake――

A poor imitation of “Natsuki Subaru”, a fake――

That thought settled into the pit of his stomach as if it were thick, black sludge.

A disgusting sludge covering him from head-to-toe; a weight that caused his knees to stop trembling. Instead, where the depths of his eyes had burned a blaze glowed, dancing atop the fuse of his dark emotions.

One could call it hostility, malice, malintent―― or perhaps an intent to kill.

Ram: [There is no way such unnatural behavior would go unnoticed, well, except for those that cannot spare the heed for it. That could be said of Julius, Anastasia-sama… well, Echidna.]

Subaru: […You’re sure letting it all out. Here I am, listening to you patiently without even saying a word.]

Ram: [――――]

Subaru: [A fake!? I think you’re crossing the line of false accusations here. Who the hell do you think you are!?]

Ram: [False accusations, huh?]

Subaru tried to voice his objections, painful as it was, in response to Ram’s unwithering sharp tongue.

Of course, the point she made had been completely correct. Even if he did not agree with having been sternly described by her as that “worthless excuse”, that unfillable void between Subaru and “Natsuki Subaru” existed.

However, she could not have noticed this. If she came to notice it, then it would become impossible for him to worm himself out of this situation.

He could not stop here, halfway through――

Subaru: [False accusations upon false accusations. My ears are deaf to them, y’know, I can’t hear any of ‘em.]

Shoulders shrugging and cheeks distorting into a scowl, Subaru spoke while glaring at Ram.

As if he were dramatizing truth and lies, which even he himself felt as to be so brazen, to be the truth of the world.

Subaru: [Anyways, what’s with going on about me having wanted to wander about in the middle of the night. I just wanted to stretch my legs and think a bit while there was no one around. That’s why I deliberately went somewhere where I wouldn’t be around your little sister’s… Rem’s sleeping, nor Patrasche’s presence…]

Ram: [――Did you really think that nobody had seen anything?]

Ram had quietly asked Subaru this as he was rapidly blurting out his excuses.

Her words were brief, but sharp, designed to puncture deep into his vitals.

Subaru: [――――]

Holding his breath at hearing her inquiry, Subaru racked his brain, trying to figure out what she had really meant with those words.

See, seen, not be seen, couldn’t have seen.

――Where did Ram get this word from, “see”. What does she mean by this?

Meili’s corpse? Or perhaps Meili, before she had become a corpse? Or had she seen him working on concealing Meili’s corpse? Or maybe――

Ram: [Nothing… even if it was your plan that nobody would see anything, the ground dragon was diligently watching your strange behavior. And because of that, Ram was also able to “watch you” in the same way.]

Putting a finger to her lips, right in front of him, Ram deliberately made a scene out of showing this particularly uncouth gesture towards Subaru.

Subaru: […What the hell are you saying!?]

Without understanding the meaning of the behavior behind her gesture, Subaru let out these words in bewilderment.

However, he did have quite a recent memory of this particular gesture. It was none other than the gesture Subaru had shown to Patrasche before leaving the Spirit room.

Right now, he could not for the life of him understand why it had cropped up again in this place with Ram right now――

Ram: [You need to study him more, you “worthless excuse”――]

The utter disappointment those two words carried, was what drove home that this was a final warning for Subaru.

Subaru: [――――]

Up until now, though extremely cold and unfeeling, Ram had kept her stance of wanting to talk, even if her intention of exchanging words with Subaru was disappearing by the second.

The instant Subaru had seen the look in her eyes, he realized that his excuses were meaningless.

Ram would turn a deaf ear to whatever excuses Subaru came up with.

Since it had come to this, Subaru had no other choice but to break through this situation using brute strength.

――In that moment, Subaru began considering how to kill Ram inside his head.

Subaru: [――――]

Think about how to kill her. There was no longer any point in feeling reluctant about it.

He had already killed one person. One or two, it made little difference. A while ago, “I” had a lot of experience in snatching away the lives of a great number of people.

From here on out, there would be no hindrance in the execution of his Book of the Dead Plan.

Meili: “――Buuut~, that onee-san… “

Countless choices within Subaru crumbled away noisily. On the verge of being left with only his dangerous thoughts, the shade of the little girl that had been keeping silent up until now chimed in.

Though invisible, the presence of the shade beside him was so strong he could practically feel her breathing. It hugged Subaru’s body from behind, softly whispering into his ear and tempting him.

And the contents of that sweet, sweet, saccharine temptation were――

Meili: “――Surely she’s in a poor condition, riiight~? Her center of gravity on the left side, its balance is poooor~.”

Those sweet whispers had brought him the weaknesses of that awe-inspiring, yet terrifying girl―― putting things short, she had given him instructions on how to kill her.

She was smaller than him, she was weaker than him, she had less experience when it came to killing.

With the knowledge “I” had accumulated by spending so much time with the assassin in black, it should be easy to kill this woman.

Meili: “Knock her over towards her left-hand side, and bash her head against the wall. That’ll do the triiick~.”

As he was taking into account the difference in strength between himself and Ram, relevant advice came from “myself”.

By just following that, the girl’s skull would crack, and flowers of deep crimson would bloom upon the wall.

Crimson flowers that would marvelously complement this lovely young girl’s pink hair――

Subaru: [――Hk!]

Without any cues, and without any obvious openings, Subaru faced his opponent in the blink of an eye, and stepped forward.

Receiving a round of cheers from the shade, Subaru stepped forwards, devoid of hesitation, towards Ram’s body which was slightly tensed. Towards her right-hand side.

He would throw her body against the wall, and then――

Ram: [Since you are lost for words, you are now resorting to violence. What a barbaric, tedious conclusion.]

In that moment of offense and defense, before Subaru’s arm could reach her, Ram had opened her lips and weaved these words.

Meeting each other’s gazes, Subaru’s breath was knocked out of him upon seeing Ram’s cold eyes.

Ram: [――Did you really think that the oh-so-frail and feeble Ram would challenge such a barbaric man alone?]

Rather than mocking him, Ram’s words felt similar to pity. As she spoke, Subaru heard a crackling sound in the air mingling in with her voice.

In fact, he was not hearing things. The moisture in the atmosphere was frozen in a flash, the air around him was being forcibly changed from gas to solid.

Subaru: [Wh… Hk.]

That crackling sound he had perceived, had been caused from Subaru’s own foot stepping against the pile of ice rising at his feet; it had stopped him in his tracks, causing him pain.

Subaru: [I-ice…!?]

Ricocheting from the hard, cold impact, Subaru could hardly believe his eyes.

But, the transformation did not just stop here. Bars of ice were not just materializing where his foot had been, rather, they rose from all around him―― enclosing him, materializing into an ice cage.

Subaru: [You’re kidding me… Hk. What the, magic…!?]

In the blink of an eye, the ice cage finished appearing in reality, within it, was Subaru completely trapped.

Seizing the bars of frost with his hands, Subaru attempted to violently shake them in an attempt to break that prison; however, his efforts only resulted in him being astounded at the strength of the ice. The bars did not yield even an inch. Even if he kicked and punched with all his strength, he did not at all believe it possible for him to be able to break through.

And thus, Subaru had been imprisoned within that ice cage――
