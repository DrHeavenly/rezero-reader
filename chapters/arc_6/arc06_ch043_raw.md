Subaru: [――――]

What had happened in this Tower? Who would kill him? And who had been killed by who.

However, of course there are exceptions.

Subaru: [The case in which the criminal was someone who’d be troubled if my memories were to come back.]

Eyewitnesses who had witnessed the murder scene lost their memory due to some sort of shock. The criminal, observing the scene, would be in trouble if their memories were to return. That was why, in the movie, the criminal tried to kill the witnesses as well.

It could definitely be thought that a similar situation was occurring to Subaru currently. Foremost, the circumstances in which Subaru had lost his memories were unclear――

Subaru: [Emilia, did you find me collapsed in the Library of the Third Floor?]

Emilia: [Yeah, that’s right. You were on the floor of the Library… Beatrice and I carried you to the Green Room in a great hurry.]

Beatrice: [Well, since it was Emilia that carried you, Betty was just there to help out behind her, in fact.]

Subaru gazed at Emilia’s slender arms as he received Beatrice’s supplementary explanation, laced with a sigh.

If he were to settle down and think properly about it, it was a mystery to Subaru how these two people had carried him all the way back from the Library to the Green Room. Beatrice did not let off a single fragment of indication that she was suited to do any heavy lifting, and from what he had been told at face value, he was also wondering how this Emilia that believed in a fake had carried Subaru so easily.

Emilia: [――? What’s wrong?]

As Subaru had thought so far, the feeling of distance between him and Emilia was a little too strong; therefore, he did not hesitate and let her touch him.

Come to think of it, when Subaru had woken up, he had also placed his hands around her neck. Nevertheless, she was looking at Subaru with a peaceful expression.

Subaru: [No matter what kind of livelihood, I will always feel a great sense of distance with this girl…]

She was likely a girl who had been sheltered as a child, unaffected by any significant hardship―― with this impression, Subaru was satisfied with this sense of distance.

If someone was not burdened with even one regret, then they could believe in only the virtue of others, and could keep your eyes all the time prim and beautiful―― Or, was even that all just an act?

Subaru: [――――]

Whilst looking at Emilia and Beatrice side-by-side, Subaru was deeply immersed within his thoughts.

Simply, when he deconstructed it down to the bare bones, when it came to Subaru’s last moments and tragedy in the Tower within the last loop, the suspects most worth keeping an eye on were Emilia and Beatrice.

Subaru had seen the corpses of Shaula, Echidna, Ram, Julius and Meili.

He had been saved by Patrasche and brought out of the lurching Tower; however, he’d had his head lopped off by someone standing behind him, and lost his life―― he had not seen the deaths of the two.

Of course, even if the two of them had conspired together, then he did not know if the possibility of the killing of the five people still remained. Subaru’s diagnosis was that it was difficult.

But, if for argument’s sake, another person existed―― if the number had increased to three, then the story would probably change.

In the midst of the chaos, in the midst of the swirl of confusion, in the midst of despairing of wanting the end to come already, he had heard this voice.

A third person cooperating with Emilia and Beatrice―― could that be the identity of an unseen third person that had carried Subaru from the Library to the Green Room? That’s why these two were spouting out such codswallop――

Subaru: [Uh, oh?]

Apparently, Subaru had walked past the room while immersed in his own thoughts. Emilia had stopped in front of the room and had placed her arm on Subaru’s shoulder as to prevent him from walking too far.

Emilia: [Ehh, but I think we’d better have it stop from being dangerous…]

There had been no sense of resistance when he had shaken her hand itself, and Emilia had shown a sense of denial to the contest of strength. Seeing this behavior, Subaru became more convinced and strongly said, “I ask this of you”.

If Emilia’s slender arm indeed did not have the strength to carry Subaru, then his deduction from before would have been given a fairly strong guarantee. So now, Emilia should not think that she was being suspected.

He would need to make best use of this advantage, a chance like this only existed right now.

Subaru: [I ask this of you, because it’s of utmost necessity.]

Receiving Subaru’s supplication, the worried Emilia pursed her lips, and gave a nod with a determined look.

Seeing that, Subaru clenched his fist with all his heart, stating “Ready”. If after, he proved her weakness, then Subaru’s deduction would progress far further.

Emilia: [Okay, got it!]

Beatrice: [Ready, go, in fact!]

Subaru: [Uohhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh!!]

It seemed as if he had yet to take an attitude which separated him from the “Natsuki Subaru” known by the girls. This made him feel relieved, and also amazed.

Doing this, he must not tip his awareness towards a different bearing, not even by a little――

The owner of that corpse moved right before his eyes, and certainly was alive.

In all honesty, from the moment he had first came across her at the hall, he had been desperate to accept a contradiction between reality and that uncomfortable feeling.
