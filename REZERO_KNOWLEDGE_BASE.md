# Re:Zero — Canonical Knowledge Base

**Purpose.** This file is the project's shared "brain" for *Re:Zero − Starting Life in Another World*: the correct English spellings of names, titles, places, and terms, each with a short description. Any node that encounters a name/term in WCT source text and is unsure of its canonical English form should recall it here first. It is a **reference**, not a rewrite mandate — how aggressively spellings are normalized is governed by the pipeline rules in `CLAUDE.md` and `GLOSSARY.md`.

**Sourcing & authority.** Compiled from the Re:Zero Wiki (rezero.fandom.com) and canonical light-novel / official-localization usage. Where the fan translation this project is built on (WCT — Witch Cult Translations) commonly renders a name differently, the WCT/variant spelling is noted as **“WCT/var.: …”** so agents can map a source spelling to its canonical form (e.g. *Pristella → Priestella*). When wiki and WCT disagree and a decision is needed for output, defer to `GLOSSARY.md` (§5.3) or flag for a human.

**How to use.** Match the source token → find the canonical spelling → apply per the active style's rules. The **Spelling reconciliation** table (bottom) is the quick lookup for the troubled renderings seen in this project so far.

## 1. Emilia Camp

- **Natsuki Subaru** — The protagonist, a Japanese high-school shut-in summoned to another world. Possesses **Return by Death**. Serves Emilia; later a Spirit Knight contracted to Beatrice.
- **Emilia** — A silver-haired half-elf and a candidate in the Royal Selection; kind, earnest, and socially isolated because she resembles the Witch of Envy. Uses the alias **Satella** early in Arc 1. Subaru's love.
- **Puck** — A grey cat-formed Great Spirit (Beast of the End); Emilia's contracted spirit and father-figure. Fire affinity.
- **Beatrice** — A small, dress-wearing Great Spirit who guards the Forbidden Archive of Roswaal's manor; centuries old. Later contracts with Subaru. Verbal tic ends many lines in “…, I suppose” (WCT: “…in fact / I suppose”; catchphrase “kashira” → “I suppose/I wonder”).
- **Rem** — A blue-haired oni maid of the Roswaal household; devoted to Subaru. Twin sister of Ram.
- **Ram** — A pink-haired oni maid; sharp-tongued, proud, devoted to Roswaal. Twin sister of Rem.
- **Roswaal L. Mathers** — Margrave of the Mathers domain and Emilia's sponsor; a flamboyant clown-painted court mage, among the most powerful magic-users alive. Drawn-out speech drawl (WCT renders elongated vowels).
- **Frederica Baumann** — A blonde, sharp-featured (fanged) head maid of the new Roswaal manor; part-beastwoman. Garfiel's older sister.
- **Petra Leyte** — A village girl of Arlam/Earlham who becomes a maid at the manor; bright and precocious.
- **Garfiel Tinsel** — A brash, fanged teenage half-beastman warrior from the Sanctuary; enormous physical strength, immature heart. Frederica's younger brother. Slang-heavy speech.
- **Otto Suwen** — A traveling merchant with the Divine Protection of hearing spirits/animals; becomes Emilia camp's harried aide-de-camp and Subaru's confidant.
- **Ryuzu** — The Ryuzu clones/original (Ryuzu Bilma / Ryuzu Meyer, etc.) tied to the Sanctuary. Small elderly-looking girl.

## 2. Other Royal Selection Candidates & Camps

- **Felt** — A blonde teenage thief from the Capital's slums; the fifth candidate, of secret royal blood. Brash, feisty.
- **Reinhard van Astrea** — Felt's knight; **the Sword Saint**, the Kingdom's strongest, blessed with countless Divine Protections. Red hair, blue eyes. *(WCT/var.: Reinhardt van Astrea.)*
- **Rom** — “Old Man Rom,” a giant who runs the slum loot house; Felt's guardian.
- **Crusch Karsten** — Duchess of House Karsten; a serious, honorable candidate wielding wind blades. Her name/existence is later devoured by Gluttony.
- **Felix Argyle** — “Ferris/Ferri,” Crusch's knight; the Kingdom's finest healer, a cat-eared young man who dresses as a girl. *(WCT often “Ferris Argyle.”)*
- **Wilhelm van Astrea** — “The Sword Devil,” Crusch's aged retainer and master swordsman; Reinhard's grandfather.
- **Anastasia Hoshin** — A merchant-guild magnate from Kararagi; ambitious candidate. Kansai-flavored dialect in Japanese (WCT renders a casual, clipped dialect). Later hosts the artificial spirit **Echidna**.
- **Julius Juukulius** — Anastasia's knight; **the Finest of Knights**, also called **the Blue Lightning**; a Spirit Knight contracted to six lesser spirits. Purple hair, yellow eyes. *(WCT/var.: Julius Euclius; epithet sometimes “the Impeccable.”)*
- **Priscilla Barielle** — A haughty, self-styled candidate who “rules by the world bending to her.”
- **Aldebaran** — “Al,” Priscilla's one-armed, helmeted knight; also seemingly from another world.
- **Ricardo Welkin**, **Mimi / Hetaro / Tivey Pearlbaton** — Anastasia's beastman mercenaries (the Iron Fang).

## 3. The Witch Cult (魔女教)

An organization, 400+ years old, that worships the Witch of Envy and seeks her resurrection. Ordinary followers at the bottom; at the top, the **Sin Archbishops**.

- **Sin Archbishop (大罪司教)** — The canonical English rank. Each Archbishop **“represents”** one of the seven sins other than Envy and wields an **Authority**. *(WCT/var.: “Cardinal of Sin,” “Bishop of [Sin].” Prefer “Sin Archbishop of [Sin]” / “representing [Sin].”)*
- **Gospel (福音)** — A book each cultist carries that shows a route to a desired future in vague hints; readable only by its owner.
- **Petelgeuse Romanée-Conti** — Sin Archbishop of **Sloth**; a gaunt, deranged fanatic obsessed with “diligence” and the Witch's love. Wields the Authority **Unseen Hand (Invisible Providence)** and body-hopping **Possession**; commands the **Fingers**. *(WCT/var.: Betelgeux / Betelgeuse.)*
- **Regulus Corneas** — Sin Archbishop of **Greed**; keeps many “wives,” entitled and self-righteous. His Authority stops his body in time (immortal/invincible) by lodging his heart in his wives; killable only by returning his heart.
- **Sirius Romanée-Conti** — Sin Archbishop of **Wrath**; wields empathy/emotional-contagion, claims to be Petelgeuse's “wife.”
- **Capella Emerada Lugunica** — Sin Archbishop of **Lust**; a transformation-inflicting dragon-blood witch. *(WCT/var.: Lugnica.)*
- **Gluttony trio** — Sin Archbishops of **Gluttony**, three siblings/aspects: **Lye Batenkaitos**, **Roy Alphard**, and **Louis Arneb**. They devour “names” and “memories,” erasing victims from the world's recollection.
- **Pride** — The Sin Archbishop seat **representing Pride is vacant** in the current era (a key fact: it is the only unfilled seat). In *Pride IF*, Subaru names himself the Sin Archbishop representing Pride.

## 4. The Witches of Sin (大罪魔女)

Nine witches, each representing a sin; active 400 years ago, six killed by Satella. Names are asteroid-derived (except Satella).

- **Satella** — Witch of **Envy** (the “Jealous Witch / Witch of Envy”); sealed, not dead. The being Emilia resembles; entwined with Return by Death.
- **Echidna** — Witch of **Greed**; a scheming intellectual obsessed with knowledge. Later persists as an artificial spirit.
- **Minerva** — Witch of **Wrath**; a healing brawler who cries over every wound she can't prevent.
- **Sekhmet** — Witch of **Sloth**; languid, immensely powerful.
- **Daphne** — Witch of **Gluttony**; creator of the Three Great Mabeasts; bound and blindfolded.
- **Typhon** — Witch of **Pride**; child-like, dispenses “judgment.”
- **Carmilla** — Witch of **Lust**; timid, self-deprecating.
- **Pandora** — Witch of **Vainglory (Vanity)**; serene manipulator of events, effectively villainous.
- **Hector** — Witch of **Melancholy/Gloom**.

## 5. Other Notable Figures

- **Elsa Granhiert** — “The **Bowel Hunter**,” a black-clad assassin who delights in disemboweling; wields kukris. *(WCT/var.: Guthunter.)*
- **Meili Portroute** — A young blue-haired mabeast-user (Witchbeast controller) working with Elsa; childish manner, twisted morals. *(WCT/var.: Mei Lee.)*
- **Reid Astrea** — The **first-generation Sword Saint**; a legendary, brutal swordsman encountered in the Pleiades Watchtower.
- **Flugel** — “The **Sage**”; legendary figure who planted the great tree and set the Watchtower's trials.
- **Shaula** — The Sage's apprentice/“Scorpion,” guardian of the Pleiades Watchtower.
- **Volcanica** — **The Dragon** of the covenant that protects the Kingdom of Lugunica.
- **Russell Fellow** — A prominent Kararagi/Lugnican merchant (referenced as an employer of shadier operatives).

## 6. Terms & Concepts

- **Return by Death (死に戻り)** — Subaru's power: upon death, time rewinds to a set “save point.” Speaking of it invites the Witch of Envy's retribution.
- **Authority (権能)** — A Witch/Witch-Factor power wielded by Witches of Sin and Sin Archbishops (e.g., Unseen Hand). Distinct from ordinary magic.
- **Witch Factor / Witch Genes (魔女因子)** — The seed that grants an Authority; determines Sin-Archbishop compatibility.
- **Divine Protection (加護, “blessing”)** — Innate god-given talents (e.g., Julius's Divine Protection of Gathering Spirits; Subaru's Witch's Lingering Scent is a curse-like counterpart).
- **Unseen Hand / Invisible Providence (見えざる手)** — Petelgeuse's Authority: invisible grasping arms.
- **Gospel (福音)** — See Witch Cult, above.
- **Miasma (瘴気)** — The Witch's foul aura; also clings to Subaru as the **Witch's Lingering Scent**, which attracts mabeasts.
- **Gate & Mana** — The body's magic organ (Gate) and the energy (mana) it processes; overuse damages the Gate.
- **Spirit Arts / Spirit Knight** — Magic performed via contracted spirits; a knight who fights with spirits (Julius; later Subaru).
- **Od Laguna** — The world's great sea/river of souls, to which the dead return.
- **The Trials (試練)** — Tests (e.g., in the Sanctuary; in the Pleiades Watchtower) that confront a challenger with past/self.
- **Cor Leonis** — The Authority (of Pride) Subaru gains in the main story (map-sense/lifeline awareness of allies). *(Context only; not present in early-Arc source.)*

## 7. Places

- **Kingdom of Lugunica (the Dragon Kingdom)** — The main setting; protected by a covenant with the Dragon Volcanica. *(WCT/var.: Lugnica.)*
- **Royal Capital Lugunica** — The capital city; site of Arc 1's loops (Shopper's district, the slums, the loot house).
- **Roswaal Manor / Mathers Domain** — Emilia camp's base; near **Arlam/Earlham Village**. *(WCT: Arlam Village.)*
- **The Sanctuary (聖域)** — A hidden settlement of half-bloods sealed by a barrier; site of Arc 4.
- **Priestella (the Water Gate City)** — One of five regional capitals, built on a lake as a flood-trap for Witches/mabeasts; site of Arc 5. *(WCT/var.: Pristella.)*
- **Pleiades Watchtower** — A tower in the **Augria Sand Dunes**; goal of Arc 6, guarded by Shaula and the Sage's trials.
- **Kararagi** — A merchant city-state confederation to the west (Anastasia's origin).
- **Vollachia (Sacred Vollachia Empire)** — The southern martial empire.
- **Gusteko (Holy Kingdom of Gusteko)** — The frozen northern theocracy.

## 8. Witchbeasts / Mabeasts (魔獣)

Beasts hostile to humanity, born of the Witch of Gluttony; obey whoever breaks their horn (or a Witchbeast-user like Meili).

- **The Three Great Mabeasts** — **White Whale** (fog that erases memory/existence), **Black Serpent**, and **Great Rabbit** (endless swarm).
- **Guiltylowe** — A ferocious black-pelted, lion-headed mabeast under Meili's command. *(WCT/var.: Guiltilaw / Guiltylawe.)*

## 9. Titles & Epithets

- **Sword Saint (剣聖)** — Reinhard van Astrea (and the hereditary Astrea title; Reid was the first).
- **Sword Devil (剣鬼)** — Wilhelm van Astrea.
- **The Finest of Knights / The Blue Lightning** — Julius Juukulius. *(WCT/var.: “the Impeccable.”)*
- **The Bowel Hunter (腸狩り)** — Elsa Granhiert. *(WCT/var.: Guthunter.)*
- **The Sage (賢者)** — Flugel.
- **The Witch of Envy / The Jealous Witch** — Satella.
- **Sin Archbishop of [Sin] / representing [Sin]** — the Witch Cult's top rank.
